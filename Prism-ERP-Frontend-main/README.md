Prism ERP ☕🥐🛍️

Prism ERP is a modern web-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system built for coffee shops, pastry businesses, and retail accessory stores. The system centralizes business operations into a single platform, allowing owners and staff to efficiently manage sales, inventory, employees, attendance, payroll, and business performance.

Inspired by enterprise solutions like SAP Business One, Prism ERP provides a clean and intuitive interface while focusing on the operational needs of small to medium-sized businesses in the food and retail industry.

    System Architecture

Prism ERP follows a modular data-driven approach where data sources are centralized into dedicated JavaScript files and synchronized across multiple modules.

Example:

sales-orders-data.js
        ↓
Sales Orders Page
        ↓
Dashboard Pending Orders
        ↓
Reports & Analytics

This architecture ensures data consistency throughout the system while keeping the codebase maintainable and scalable.
